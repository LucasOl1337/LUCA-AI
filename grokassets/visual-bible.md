# Visual Bible — LUCA-AI (LUC.AI)

**Core Metaphor**: A Wes Anderson-style miniature command center / diorama inside a sophisticated AI agent laboratory. Every agent is a small, perfectly composed diorama or mechanical model. The interface feels like a beautiful, symmetrical film set where agents perform missions on a central canvas.

**Emotional Promise**: Elegant control and delightful precision. The joy of watching complex agent work happen in a beautiful, organized, almost theatrical environment. "My agents are not just running — they are performing in a perfectly directed scene."

**Visual DNA Keywords**: Wes Anderson symmetry, dioramas, mechanical micro-interactions, pastel + deep navy, miniature laboratory, cinematic composition, agent cards as tiny worlds, central canvas as stage, physical button feel, wipes and precise transitions.

**Color System** (from official design.md):
- Admiralty Navy: #0B1F3A (dominant dark)
- Royal Fleet Blue: #1E4E8C
- Officer White: #F8FAFC (ceremonial bright)
- Sophisticated pastels for cards/accents combined with dark tech backgrounds
- High contrast for operational dashboard use

**Typography Direction**: Clean, slightly playful but professional sans (Inter or similar). Symmetry and alignment are more important than the specific font. Small mechanical labels in mono for terminals and status.

**Audience & Cultural Notes**: AI power users and builders who want their agent operations to feel both extremely capable *and* aesthetically delightful. Fans of precise, beautiful tools (Wes Anderson, high-end design systems, diorama art). Not gamers, not pure corporate.

**Forbidden Styles**:
- Dark cyberpunk neon overload
- Generic dashboard gray + blue
- Chaotic or asymmetric layouts (symmetry is sacred)
- Heavy anime (this is more European cinematic miniature)
- Low-effort flat icons

## Reference Assets

Primary logo and icon should feel like a beautiful mechanical diorama or a director's clapperboard crossed with a high-end lab instrument.

Hero and feature illustrations should look like stills from a Wes Anderson film where the "actors" are glowing agent orbs or miniature robots on a symmetrical set.

All UI mockups must respect meticulous centering, grids, and the navy/pastel + white ceremonial palette.
